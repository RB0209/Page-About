About CARE Foundation

Empowering communities through education, awareness, and compassion since 2011.

Our Mission

Founded in Mumbai in 2011 by a group of passionate youngsters, CARE Foundation has emerged as a beacon of hope and change in the realm of social awareness, education, and women empowerment. This non-governmental organization (NGO) is dedicated to addressing pressing societal issues through innovative programs and deep community engagement.

What We Stand For

Education

Creating accessible learning opportunities for underprivileged youth and children.

Empowerment

Supporting women and communities to become self-reliant through training and awareness.

Healthcare

Running health drives, hygiene camps, and mental wellness initiatives for all ages.
