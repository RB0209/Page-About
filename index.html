About Us

Founded in Mumbai in 2011 by a group of passionate youngsters, CARE Foundation has emerged as a beacon of hope and change in the realm of social awareness, education, and women empowerment. This Non-governmental organization (NGO) has dedicated itself to addressing pressing societal issues through innovative programs and community engagement.

Our Mission

To empower underprivileged communities through education, healthcare, and social upliftment programs that foster sustainable development.

Our Vision

To build a just, equitable, and compassionate society where every individual has access to basic rights and opportunities to flourish.
